About Biodanza

Biodanza, literally the “dance of life,” was created in the 1960s by Chilean anthropologist Rolando Toro. It is a form of self‑development that weaves together music, movement and emotional expression to help us reconnect with ourselves and each other. Sessions are crafted to stimulate the joy of being, promote internal integration, recover essential instincts and the drivers of life force, and to reconnect us as human beings. Facilitators skilfully blend rhythmic to restful music with dance invitations and group encounters that honour our human physiology. These intense experiences — known as vivencias — help us fall in love with life and explore five primary lines of living: vitality, creativity, affectivity, sensuality and transcendence.

“Biodanza is the bridge between my body and my truth.”
